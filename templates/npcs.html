Key characters and their known agendas in the world of ice and fire

Lord Frostcrown

Iceborn Warlords
Known Agenda

Seeks to unite the northern clans under the banner of eternal winter. Believes the Everfrost is a blessing, not a curse.

What the Party Knows
  • Commands the Ice Guard
  • Has dealings with frost cultists
  • Hostile to fire elementalists

Ember Blackforge

Ashblood Legion
Known Agenda

Master smith seeking to reforge the Sunforged weapons. Believes fire magic can push back the eternal winter.

What the Party Knows
  • Expert in ancient metallurgy
  • May have Sunforged fragments
  • Seeking rare fire crystals

Seer Whitevision

Frostborn Cult
Known Agenda

Mysterious prophet who claims to have visions of the true prophecy. Her loyalties remain unclear.

What the Party Knows
  • Speaks in riddles and metaphors
  • Has knowledge of ancient events
  • Cannot be trusted completely

Rumors & Player Notes

  • Rumored to be seeking ancient ice artifacts
  • Some say he's not entirely human anymore
  • Player theory: Connected to the prophecy somehow

  • Her forge never seems to go cold despite the Everfrost
  • May have connections to the Keepers of the Last Ember
  • Player theory: She knows more about the prophecy than she lets on
