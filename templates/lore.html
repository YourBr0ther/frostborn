Cultural Lore

Khione, the Frost Mother

Goddess of winter, preservation, and the eternal cycle. Her followers believe the Everfrost is her blessing upon the world.

Ignis, the Flame Eternal

God of fire, passion, and change. His priests maintain that only flame can bring life and prevent stagnation.

For millennia, the world existed in perfect balance between frost and fire. Neither element dominated completely, creating seasons, weather patterns, and the natural order of life and death.

The Summoning shattered this balance, and now the world tilts toward eternal winter. Some seek to restore the balance, others to tip it toward flame, and still others believe a new equilibrium must be forged.

Magic flows from the fundamental forces of creation - primarily frost and fire, but also the spaces between them. As the Everfrost spreads, ice magic grows stronger while fire magic requires greater effort and rare components.

  • Frost Magic: Preservation, slowing, crystallization
  • Fire Magic: Transformation, acceleration, purification
  • Life Magic: The rare balance between elements
