House Rules

Cold Weather Effects
  • Extreme Cold: Constitution saves every hour in Everfrost territory
  • Fire Magic Bonus: +1 to fire spell attack rolls and damage in cold areas
  • Ice Magic Enhancement: Ice/frost spells deal +2 damage in Everfrost regions
  • Warmth Sources: Magical fires prevent cold exhaustion for the group
Combat Modifications
  • Inspiration Usage: Can be spent after seeing the roll result
  • Flanking: Grants advantage when positioning allows
  • Elemental Weaknesses: Fire creatures take +50% cold damage, ice creatures take +50% fire damage
Roleplay Encouragement
  • Character Voices: Bonus inspiration for staying in character
  • Creative Solutions: Non-combat resolutions often yield better rewards
  • Teamwork Bonus: Group tactics and coordination provide mechanical benefits

Warmth & Shelter
  • Shelter Priority: Always seek shelter before nightfall in Everfrost areas
  • Fire Maintenance: Requires fuel every 4 hours or magical enhancement
  • Group Warmth: Sharing body heat provides advantage on cold saves
  • Frost-Proof Gear: Prevents equipment from freezing and breaking
Hazards to Watch For
  • Frost mirages that lead travelers astray
  • Avalanche zones in mountainous regions
  • Thin ice over hidden crevasses
  • Frost storms that reduce visibility to zero

Session Zero Agreements

Tone & Themes
  • Overall Tone: Epic fantasy with moments of hope in dark times
  • Themes: Balance, sacrifice, the cost of power, redemption
  • Content Rating: PG-13 - mature themes but not graphic violence
  • Humor: Welcome but shouldn't undermine serious moments
Safety Tools
  • X-Card: Anyone can pause/skip content that makes them uncomfortable
  • Lines: Content we absolutely avoid (set in private with DM)
  • Veils: Content that happens 'off-screen' if it comes up
  • Open Door: Players can step out if needed, no questions asked
Table Expectations
  • Respect everyone at the table
  • Share spotlight with other players
  • Communicate openly about concerns
  • Support each other's storytelling
  • Phones on silent during sessions
  • Give 24hrs notice for absence
  • Come prepared (character sheet, dice, etc.)
  • Rotate snack/drink responsibility
