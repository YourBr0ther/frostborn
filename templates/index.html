Campaign Overview

The world stands on the precipice of eternal winter. The Everfrost spreads across the land, consuming everything in its path, while ancient powers stir in the depths of ice and flame.

In this realm where the balance between frost and fire determines the fate of all living things, our heroes must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover forgotten prophecies, and make choices that will echo through the ages. Will they embrace the cold embrace of the Everfrost, seek to rekindle the dying flames, or forge a new path between ice and fire?

The prophecy speaks of chosen ones who will either herald the world's salvation or its final doom. The question remains: which destiny will our heroes choose?
